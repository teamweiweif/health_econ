## Interpretation

- ALB_2005 has observable raw OOP payment variables in `Modul_9A_healtha_cl.sav`, but they are split between four-week payment/gift items and twelve-month hospital/dentist payment/gift items.
- Gift variables are present in both recall windows. Their inclusion in OOP health expenditure requires a documented policy definition and missing-code review before any CHE or SDG 3.8.2 outcome is promoted.
- Several access variables in `Modul_9B_healthb_cl.sav` have useful value labels for cost, distance, refusal, and medicine-discount barriers, but many are conditional reason variables and cannot define denominators without questionnaire skip-pattern review.
- ALB_2005 still lacks verified interview timing and full-coverage geography, so climate linkage remains blocked even if outcome semantics improve.
